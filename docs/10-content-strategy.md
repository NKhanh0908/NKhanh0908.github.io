# Content Strategy

## Purpose

This portfolio is not intended to function as a traditional resume.

Instead, it presents the journey of a developer who enjoys understanding systems, solving problems, and building software that creates real value.

The experience should feel thoughtful, structured, and professional.

Visitors should naturally discover who the developer is, what they have built, and how they approach engineering challenges.

---

# Core Narrative

The entire website follows a simple progression:

Curiosity

↓

Each section represents a stage in the evolution of a software engineer.

# Communication Principles

### Show, Don't Tell

Avoid statements such as:

* I am passionate
* I am hardworking
* I am dedicated

Instead:

* Show projects
* Show systems
* Show experience
* Show decisions

---

### Engineering First

Focus on:

* System thinking
* Architecture
* Security
* Scalability
* Maintainability

rather than flashy visuals.

---

### Simplicity Wins

Visitors should understand:

* Who you are
* What you build
* Why it matters

within the first 30 seconds.

---

# Key Messages

The visitor should leave with the following impressions:

* This developer understands backend systems.
* This developer has built real applications.
* This developer values maintainability and architecture.
* This developer continues learning and improving.
